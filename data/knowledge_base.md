# AutoStream – Pricing, Features & Policies

AutoStream is an AI-powered video editing SaaS platform designed for content creators.

---

## 🔹 Pricing Plans

### 🟢 Basic Plan
- Price: $29 per month
- Video Limit: 10 videos per month
- Resolution: 720p (HD)
- Best for: Beginners and casual creators

---

### 🔵 Pro Plan
- Price: $79 per month
- Video Limit: Unlimited videos
- Resolution: 4K (Ultra HD)
- Features:
  - AI-generated captions
  - Faster rendering
  - Priority processing
- Support: 24/7 customer support included
- Best for: Professional creators and agencies

## 🔹 Company Policies

- Refund Policy:
  - Refunds are available only within 7 days of purchase
  - No refunds after 7 days

- Support Policy:
  - Basic Plan: Standard support
  - Pro Plan: 24/7 priority support

## 🔹 Common Questions

### Q: Which plan is best for YouTube creators?
A: The Pro Plan is recommended because it supports 4K resolution and unlimited video generation.

### Q: Does AutoStream support captions?
A: Yes, AI-generated captions are included in the Pro Plan.

### Q: Can I upgrade my plan later?
A: Yes, you can upgrade from Basic to Pro at any time.
